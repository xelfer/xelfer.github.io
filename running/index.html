Race Day Strategy

Start-10km 6:45/km or slower — VERY conservative. Adrenaline + crowd will pull you forward. Hold back hard.
10-20km settle into a comfortable rhythm (~6:30/km if it feels easy). Conversational.
20-32km the hills hit here. Stay relaxed on climbs, recover on downhills. Effort over pace.
32-42km whatever's left. If you've paced right, keep rolling. If not, keep moving — finishing is the win.
HR cap stay under 165 until 30km, then let it climb.
Fuel gel every 30-40min from 5km. ~6-7 gels total. Rehearse on long runs.
Walk through aid stations for water — saves energy, costs ~5 sec each.
If MP work never fully came online start even more conservatively and run purely by feel. A comfortable finish beats a blown-up time chase.
